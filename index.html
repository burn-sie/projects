Excel Tools: A Monthly Watchbill Generator

Along time ago i was in the navy and thought to myself, "there has to be a better way to do some of the scheduling around here".
My first thought was Excel of course as it was the only thing i kinda really knew how to use. Then i thought, why create something from scratch? I'm sure someone has thought of a way to do this already. So went ahead and searched the internet only to be left disappointed. I ran to the usual links everyone runs into, which i will post here hopefully will help people land on this page.

01. USNI Encourage Small-Scale Software Innovation
02. An old picture of a watchbill from some random ROTC probably
03. An old forum post asking for help with VBA code. (Unsafe)
04. Another Forum post with a working watchbill but its old
05. The navy has thought about this already but said program MIA

Eventually i decided that i wasnt going to find anything useful on the net, so i created a large tool set of excel sheets to help in various ways, from planning, tracking when things were due, to inventory sheet. Here is the first of them.

I created most of these tools from scratch on my own time and i adapted them to fit my needs then and they are still helpful in my needs now when i need something quick.
